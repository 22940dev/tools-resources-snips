This is currently an extension of my wiki. A place where I keep snippets of code/notes/config. As well as a place for deep thinking and connecting knowledge in meaningful ways. All done with a goal to create a digital version of my brain that I can seamlessly query and gain insight from.

It's using Dendron to make that happen.

As I am working on Dendron to make it better for everyone, I will also use this as a place to conduct thought experiments. Inspired greatly by Andy and the many wikis others haved shared.

The space of digital gardens is still small and the tools are getting better. Dendron, Obsidian, Roam, RemNote, Sublime Text..

Gardens unlike blogs don't go stale and are easier to keep vibrant and grow organically. Take a look around and I hope I can inspire you to make your own digital garden, where you share what you know. I'd love to read it. ðŸŒ±

The source of this garden is on GitHub.
